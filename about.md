Hello. I am Davide Bolognini, a bioinformatician working in computational genomics. I’m specialized in algorithms for sequence analysis and variants discovery. I love programming and most of my code is publicly available on [GitHub](https://github.com/davidebolo1993). I work at the [University of Florence](https://www.unifi.it/changelang-eng.html) and I have collaborations with [EMBL](https://www.embl.de), an outstanding research institution for molecular biology. My publications are listed on [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=-MqrVqkAAAAJ), [Web of Science](https://publons.com/researcher/3146528/davide-bolognini) and [ORCID](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8735-8093). If you are looking for help with one of my softwares either you have a feature request, feel free to get in touch with me at <davidebolognini7@gmail.com> or to open a GitHub issue. Meetings can be scheduled through [Calendly](https://calendly.com/davidebolognini7).
